Filter rows from the list

Enter the text to search for that will match any position in the string.
The special characters you can use are:
- "*": Defines any character or group of characters.
- "|": Use this symbol to separate one of several texts to search.
- "^": If the text begins with "^", it indicates that the cell should begin with the indicated string.
- "$": If the text begins with "$", it indicates that the cell must end with the indicated string.
- "=": If the text starts with "=", the search will be limited to text that exactly matches the entire cell. It is similar to starting with "^" and ending with "$".
